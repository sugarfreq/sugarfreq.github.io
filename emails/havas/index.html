Dear Colleague,

Medical cannabis is here. No matter where you stand on the issue, it’s becoming a relevant and important topic. Your patients want to talk about it. Increase your knowledge of the science of medical cannabis and address concerns surrounding liability.  Join the conversation. You, and your patients, will be glad you did.

Dedicated to endocannabinoid science and education, Havas ECS is partnering with the American Journal of Endocannabinoid Medicine for a first-of-its-kind medical conference.

This one-day conference will cover the high science of the endocannabinoid system, drug and disease interactions and risks, uses, labeling and delivery systems.

Please join me and my fellow speakers on November 15 in New York as we begin to untangle the science, the research and the risks of endocannabinoids.
